Player Races

Name About
Human Humans have no particular strengths or weaknesses. Humans are everywhere, doing everything.
Fox Foxes are cunning, predatory canines. Their intelligence is high, but they have reduced defense.
Raccoon Raccoons are sneaky bandit-masked tricksters, who are very skillful, but not as strong as other races.
Owl Owls are large-eyed birds with excellent perception, but their wing-hands gives them less skill.
Wolf Wolves are huge canine creatures, the strongest of all races, but their size makes it easier for them to miss small details.
Ox The horned, hooved oxes are the largest race, and do not tire easily. They have a reputation for slow minds, however.
Rabbit Rabbits are small and skillful, with long ears that are always alert. They are less sturdy than most, however.
Sheep Sheep are nervous wooly horned creatures who's anxiety makes them miss a lot, but they are hardier than they look.
